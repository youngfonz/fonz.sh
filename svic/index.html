The Honest Bracket 09 / 16

But —

Some things, you don't delegate.

  1. The 1:1 where someone tells you they're struggling.
  2. The hard feedback — the kind that's about them, not the work.
  3. The apology. Public or private.
  4. The "well done" that lands because they know you actually saw it.

You can't ChatGPT a human being.

Judgment · Presence · Care Pillar 2 · The other half
The Other Half 15 / 27

And —

Everything else? Delegate.

  1. The first draft of any memo, brief, or doc.
  2. The forty pages your team would've read to find the one paragraph that matters.
  3. The Monday status report. The dashboard pull. The board pre-read.
  4. The customer ticket that has the same six answers, rotated.

Stop being precious about work that wasn't valuable when you did it.

The fastest way to free your judgment is to stop spending it on the obvious Pillar 2 · What to let go of
