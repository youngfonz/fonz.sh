// 01   THE STUDIO PLAY SIGNAL FLOW × LEGENDS

Let It Flow.

A grid puzzle built on signal flow: the studio fundamental behind every track Erick Sermon, Dr. Dre, or Pharrell ever made. Route INPUT to SPEAKER. Five sessions, five studios, five artists. Earn Plaques on a shareable wall.

  • Real audio engineering. The puzzle teaches actual studio thinking, not gamified fluff
  • Star-power anchor: we'd target a producer of Erick Sermon's caliber to attach a name
  • Plaque Wall drives replay and sharing
  • Most achievable scope. Grid puzzle is well-understood

// 02   THE BRAND STORY NOVA  ×  ECHO

Rhythm & Rescue.

A Wizard of Static erased all music. Nova, a young Black girl producer, runs, jumps, and plays the beat to bring it back. Side-scroller meets rhythm game. ECHO, her scripted companion, guides with lifeline hints.

  • Single-realm MVP (Rhythm Ruins). Fits the playable format. Realms 2 + 3 ship as updates
  • Nova is owned IP. Built once, used across BGC media for years
  • ECHO is a scripted character (not live AI). Teaches about AI from inside the game world
  • Real skill: rhythm, timing, listening. Actual music education

// 03   THE VIRAL HOOK HUMAN × AI

Human or AI?

Hear a 10-second clip. Human or AI? 8-second timer. Pre-curated clips from real artists and AI models. Pre-written tells reveal the giveaway after every guess.

  • "I got 8/10. Can you beat me?" is a YouTube-native share format
  • Teaches AI literacy without using live AI. Constraint becomes the game
  • Weekly clip drops keep the game fresh and the algorithm fed
  • Real skill: critical listening, source skepticism, ear training
