Rhythm & Rescue

Play as Nova, a young Black girl producer. Rebuild songs. Bring music back.

Kids don't learn music here.
They make it — and learning happens because they did.

What it is

The Wizard of Static erased all music — the world has gone gray and silent. As Nova, you rebuild songs to bring music, color, and life back. ECHO, a scripted companion (not live AI), guides you and quietly teaches what AI really is: a tool that helps — you are the creator.

How you play — one loop, three taps

🎮
Play

Beat Run

Tap falling tiles in time — like Magic Tiles — to perform Nova's beat. Fun in 5 seconds.

🎧
Learn

Sound Check

ECHO names the parts of that beat. Learning each word unlocks the instrument.

🎚️
Create

Beat Builder

Stack the parts onto a grid to make your own version. The world comes alive to the music you made.

Build order: Drums → Bass → Trumpet → Vocals
No “Game Over” — it's no-fail. Challenge is opt-in.

How we build it

It looks big — three realms, a story, an ending — but runs on one small shared engine: a single clock, audio engine, and beat that every mode is just a view on. Realm 1 ships first and stands alone. Realms 2 & 3 are mostly content drops on the same engine — that's how we dream big and ship lean. Built in HTML5 + Web Audio, sized for Playables.

Roadmap: Core engine → Beat Run (the playable hook) → Realm 1 full loop (the MVP) → polish & submit → Realm 2 → Realm 3 + finale + a free-play Studio sandbox.

The win & the ending

Restore all three realms, then perform Nova's complete song to push back the Static. Music floods back, Nova takes the stage, and you unlock the Studio — make your own songs with every sound you collected. You win by creating, not by being perfect.
