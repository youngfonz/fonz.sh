# Rhythm & Rescue — One-Pager

**A YouTube Playables music game by Black Girls Code.** Selected by YouTube. Status: in design.

> **Kids don't learn music here. They *make* it — and learning happens because they did.**

---

### What it is
Play as **Nova**, a young Black girl producer. The **Wizard of Static** erased all music and the
world has gone gray and silent. You rebuild songs to bring music — and color, motion, and life —
back. **ECHO**, a scripted companion (not live AI), guides you and quietly teaches what AI really
is: *a tool that helps; you are the creator.*

### How you play — one loop, three taps
**Play → Learn → Create**, all on one finger:

1. **PLAY** — *Beat Run*: tap falling tiles in time (think *Magic Tiles*) to perform Nova's beat. Fun in 5 seconds.
2. **LEARN** — *Sound Check*: ECHO names the parts of that beat; learning each word **unlocks** the instrument.
3. **CREATE** — *Beat Builder*: stack the parts onto a grid — **drums → bass → trumpet → vocals** — to make your own version.

The world comes alive to the music **you** made. No "Game Over" — it's no-fail; challenge is opt-in.

### Why it matters
- **Real skill:** rhythm, timing, layering, arrangement — actual music education, taught by doing.
- **Representation:** a girl who looks like the player makes music and runs the show.
- **Owned IP:** Nova is built once and reused across BGC media for years.
- **Safe by design:** scripted (no live AI), no accounts, no data collection — built to pass kids-safety review.

### How we build it (ambitious vision, disciplined build)
The game *looks* big — 3 realms, a story, an ending — but runs on **one small shared engine**:
a single clock, audio engine, and beat that every mode is just a *view* on. **Realm 1 ships first
and stands alone.** Realms 2 & 3 are mostly content drops on the same engine — that's why we can
dream big and ship lean. Built in HTML5 + Web Audio, sized for Playables.

**Roadmap:** Core engine → **Beat Run** (the playable hook) → Realm 1 full loop (the MVP) →
polish & submit → Realm 2 → Realm 3 + finale + a free-play **Studio** sandbox.

### The win & the ending
Restore all three realms, then perform Nova's complete song to push back the Static. The ending:
music floods back, Nova takes the stage, and you unlock the **Studio** — make your own songs with
every sound you collected. *You win by creating, not by being perfect.*
