The Five Stages

One screen per stage. Each one adds a layer to the beat. These are interface mockups to show the team what playing each stage looks like.

Stage 1 · Rhythm Ruins
The Pulse
ECHO: Tap the tiles when they hit the line. Give the world a heartbeat.
KickSnareKickSnare
1The Pulse — Drums Falling tiles, 4 lanes (kick/snare). Tap in time. Same idea as Magic Tiles. Unlocks the drums.
Stage 2 · Groove River
The Groove
ECHO: Tap the buttons to build a bassline. The drums keep playing under you.
Drums (playing)
Bass (you tap)
2The Groove — Bass Row of 8 buttons. Tap to drop in bass notes; a marker sweeps across and your pattern loops. Every note is in key. Unlocks the bass.

3The Color — Chords Three pads: Low, Mid, High. Press combos to play chords over the beat. The right one floods the world with color. Unlocks the chords.
Stage 4 · Melody Heights
The Melody
ECHO: Five notes now. Tap the tiles and play the part you'll be humming later.
doremisola
4The Melody — Trumpet Falling tiles again, but 5 lanes — each a different note. Every tap is a real note, so it always sounds musical. Unlocks the melody.
