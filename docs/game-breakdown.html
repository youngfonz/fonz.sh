Section 1
The Pulse
Drums — kick + snare
+Drums

The Wizard of Static erased everything. The world is gray and silent. Nova enters the Rhythm Ruins — an ancient stage where the drum machine sits broken. To bring the pulse back, she has to play it.

Mechanic
Beat Run — falling tile rhythm game (Magic Tiles style)
4 lanes: Kick · Snare · Kick · Snare
What the player does
Tap the falling tiles in time. Visual column lights up when tapped. Drum sound plays on each hit.
Win condition
Complete the phrase — reach the end of the pattern. No fail state. Missing tiles just don't sound.
Dev spec
Canvas or DOM tiles. One 2-bar loop. 4 columns. Tiles timed to BPM grid. ~90 BPM. Kick one-shot + snare one-shot on tap.
ECHO says —
"The world needs a heartbeat before it can feel anything else. You just gave it one."
🔓 Drum loop unlocked. World background gets a pulse animation — a slow rhythmic glow that beats in time with the drums now playing.

Section 2
The Groove
Bass — low-end foundation
+Bass

The world has a heartbeat now but it feels hollow. Nova finds a bass amp buried in rubble. The Wizard sealed the bass away — she has to build a bassline to break it free.

Mechanic
Beat Builder (bass only) — 8-step grid, one row
Drums play automatically underneath while player builds
What the player does
Tap any of the 8 steps to place a bass note. Hear the pattern loop in real time as they build it. Each tapped step plays a pentatonic bass note.
Win condition
Fill at least 4 of the 8 steps. No wrong notes (pentatonic = everything sounds good). ECHO then plays the "rescued" bass stem on top.
Dev spec
8 tappable cells, one row. Toggle on/off. 2-bar loop playback at BPM. Bass one-shots mapped to pentatonic scale. Playhead sweeps across. Drums loop continues from Section 1.
ECHO says —
"That's YOUR bassline. That's what you made. Now hear what the Wizard tried to erase."
[Full bass stem fades in on top of player's pattern]
🔓 Bass stem unlocked. World visual gets depth — shadows and ground appear. Drums + bass now play together.
↓
🎹
Section 3
The Color
Chords — harmonic warmth
+Chords

Pulse. Groove. But still gray and cold. Nova finds a keyboard half-buried in static. Harmony is what gives music its emotion — to bring color back to the world, she has to find the right chord.

Mechanic
Chord Rescue — press and hold combinations of 3 color-coded buttons (Low · Mid · High)
Like finding a lock combination by feel
What the player does
Three large buttons on screen (Low / Mid / High). Press any combo — a chord plays over the drums + bass. When they find the "resonant" chord, the world reacts with color and warmth.
Win condition
Land on any one of the 3 pre-set resonant chords. All combos sound musical (pentatonic). Some just "light up the world" — visual + audio reward. Can't lose, just exploring.
Dev spec
3 buttons. 7 possible presses (3 singles + 3 pairs + 1 all-three). Map these to 7 pentatonic chord voicings. Mark 3 as "resonant" = trigger world animation. No timer. Pure feel.
ECHO says —
"Chords are the colors of music. Same notes. Different order. Completely different feeling. You just felt that."
🔓 Chord stem unlocked. World gets color and light — warm gradients flood in. Drums + bass + chords now all play.
↓
🎺
Section 4
The Melody
Trumpet / Lead — the part you hum
+Melody

The world has rhythm, groove, and warmth — but still no melody. No hook. No song. Nova finds a trumpet that hasn't made a sound since the Static arrived. The melody is the part that gets stuck in your head. She has to play it to remember it.

Mechanic
Melody Run — Beat Run variant with 5 color-coded columns (one per pentatonic note)
Every column = a real note. Any tile you tap plays music.
What the player does
Tiles fall across 5 lanes (colored by pitch: low → high). Tap in time to hit the melody. Even if they miss, tapping any lane plays a pentatonic note — nothing sounds wrong.
Win condition
Reach the end of the melodic phrase. Hit rate shown as a score (optional, not required to advance). Full trumpet stem plays as reward regardless.
Dev spec
Same tile engine as Beat Run (Section 1) — reuse it, change column count from 4 → 5. Map lanes to pentatonic pitches. Tile sequence encodes the melody. Trumpet one-shots per pitch on tap.
ECHO says —
"That's the melody — the part you'll be humming tomorrow. The Wizard tried to make you forget it. You remembered it."
🔓 Lead melody unlocked. World gets movement and life — leaves, particles, animated characters. Drums + bass + chords + trumpet now play.
↓
🎤
Section 5
The Voice
Vocals — the human story
+Vocals

Everything is there — but still no words. No voice. The Wizard scattered the lyrics across the world. Words are what turn a beat into a song. Nova has to find them and put them back together.

Mechanic
Lyric Rescue — fragments of the vocal hook drift/fall across the screen
Tap each word in the right order to rebuild the lyric line
What the player does
4–6 word tiles float around the screen (example: "Let · the · music · find · you"). Tap them in order to assemble the line. Each correct tap plays that syllable of the vocal. Completion plays the full hook.
Win condition
Assemble all lyric fragments in order. Wrong taps just don't register (no punishment). Full beat plays when the last word clicks into place. This is the first time the player hears all 5 layers together.
Dev spec
Floating/drifting word elements (CSS animation or Canvas). Tap-to-collect. Order validation. On completion: play full 5-stem mix for first time. ECHO's "you did it" transition. Advance to Finale.
ECHO says —
"Words make music into stories. You didn't just find those words — you chose to put them back together. That's what creators do."
🔓 Vocal stem unlocked. Full song plays for the first time. All 5 layers. The world is almost fully restored. Finale triggers.

Developer Notes

One shared engine Single clock drives all sections. Beat Run tiles, Beat Builder playhead, and Lyric Rescue all sync to the same BPM transport. Build the transport first — everything plugs into it.
Sections 1 + 4 reuse the same tile engine Beat Run (Section 1) uses 4 lanes. Melody Run (Section 4) uses 5 lanes. Same renderer, different column count and audio map.
Audio layers accumulate Each section's stem stays playing after unlock. By Section 5, all 5 stems are looping simultaneously. Web Audio API gain nodes — each stem gets its own GainNode for mute/unmute in the finale.
No fail states anywhere Players advance by completing the activity, not by accuracy. Drums: reach the end. Bass: fill 4+ steps. Chords: find any resonant combo. Melody: reach the end. Vocals: assemble in order.
Pentatonic = no wrong notes Every tap in every section plays a pentatonic pitch. Impossible to sound bad. Players can smash randomly and it will still be musical. This is intentional — removes fear, increases experimentation.
All you need from audio production One BPM, one key. Four stems (drums / bass / chords / lead / vocals) as 2-bar WAV loops. One-shots per instrument (kick, snare, hat, 3–5 bass notes, 5 lead pitches, lyric syllables).
