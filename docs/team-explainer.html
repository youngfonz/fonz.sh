4 / 14
🎤
Nova
Our hero. Young Black girl producer. BGC-owned IP.
Nova makes music. She's not a sidekick — she is the creator. When the Wizard of Static erases all music from the world, Nova goes section by section to bring it back using her skills as a producer.

She looks like the kids playing this game. That's intentional and it matters.

Nova belongs to BGC. We build her once and use her everywhere — events, curriculum, media, future games.
↓
5 / 14
🤖
ECHO
Nova's companion. Scripted — not live AI.
ECHO lives in the game world and quietly teaches kids what AI actually is — a tool that helps, not something that replaces you.

ECHO never solves anything for Nova. ECHO echoes — names what Nova just did, celebrates her choices. The player is always the one making music.

No live AI. No generation. Pre-written lines only. Safe for kids, safe for YouTube.

The Story

The Wizard of Static erased all music. The world is gray and silent. Nova must travel through five broken sections of the world, rescue one piece of music from each, and put them all together to bring music back.

↓
7 / 14
Section 1 — Rhythm Ruins
01
The Pulse
Drums
What you do
Beat Run — tap falling tiles in time to the drum beat. Like Magic Tiles. Four lanes: kick · snare · kick · snare.
🔓 Drums unlocked — world gets a heartbeat
↓
8 / 14
Section 2 — Groove River
02
The Groove
Bass
What you do
Beat Builder — tap an 8-step grid to place bass notes. Drums play underneath. Hear your pattern loop in real time. Can't sound bad — every note is in key.
🔓 Bass unlocked — world gets foundations
↓
9 / 14
Section 3 — Chord Fields
03
The Color
Chords
What you do
Chord Rescue — press combinations of three buttons (Low · Mid · High) to make chords over the beat. When you land on the right chord, the world fills with color.
🔓 Chords unlocked — world fills with color
↓
10 / 14
Section 4 — Melody Heights
04
The Melody
Trumpet
What you do
Melody Run — falling tiles, 5 lanes, each a different pitch. Every tap plays a real note. Even tapping randomly sounds musical.
🔓 Melody unlocked — world comes to life
↓
11 / 14
Section 5 — Static Citadel
05
The Voice
Vocals
What you do
Lyric Rescue — word fragments drift across the screen. Tap them in order to rebuild the vocal hook. When the last word clicks — the full song plays for the first time.
🔓 Vocals unlocked — full song plays

12 / 14
The Finale

Nova
Performs

All 5 layers appear. You can remix — mute and unmute each one. Then you hit Perform. The Static dissolves. Color floods back. Nova takes the stage. Music is back.

ECHO's final line
"I only echoed what was already in you.
You rescued the music. But you made it yours."
