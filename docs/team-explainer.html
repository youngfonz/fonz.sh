🎤
Nova
Our hero. A young Black girl who produces music.
Nova is the one making the music. When the Wizard of Static wipes out every sound in the world, she's the one who goes out and brings it back, section by section.

The kid playing the game sees a producer who looks like them, running the whole show. That's the point.

She's a character we created, so she can show up in our other work down the line too.
↓
5 / 14
🤖
ECHO
Nova's companion. Scripted, not live AI.
ECHO travels with Nova and helps kids understand what AI actually is: a tool that helps you, not something that does the work for you.

ECHO never makes the music. It points out what the player just did and cheers them on. The kid is always the one in control.

Every line ECHO says is written ahead of time. No live AI in the game, which keeps it safe for kids and easy to clear with YouTube.

The Story

The Wizard of Static wiped out all the music. The world went gray and quiet. Nova travels through five parts of that world, brings back one piece of music in each, and puts them together into a full song.

↓
7 / 14
Section 1 — Rhythm Ruins
01
The Pulse
Drums
What you do
Beat Run. Tap falling tiles in time with the beat, like Magic Tiles. Four lanes: kick, snare, kick, snare.
🔓 You unlock the drums. The world gets a heartbeat.
↓
8 / 14
Section 2 — Groove River
02
The Groove
Bass
What you do
Beat Builder. Tap an 8-step grid to drop in bass notes while the drums play under you. Your pattern loops back so you hear it as you go. Every note is in key, so it always sounds good.
🔓 You unlock the bass. The world gets some weight to it.
↓
9 / 14
Section 3 — Chord Fields
03
The Color
Chords
What you do
Chord Rescue. Press combinations of three buttons (Low, Mid, High) to play chords over the beat. Land on the right one and the world fills with color.
🔓 You unlock the chords. Color comes back.
↓
10 / 14
Section 4 — Melody Heights
04
The Melody
Trumpet
What you do
Melody Run. Falling tiles again, but now 5 lanes, each one a different note. Every tap is a real note, so even random taps sound like music.
🔓 You unlock the melody. The world starts moving.
↓
11 / 14
Section 5 — Static Citadel
05
The Voice
Vocals
What you do
Lyric Rescue. Words drift across the screen. Tap them in order to put the hook back together. Once the last word lands, the whole song plays for the first time.
🔓 You unlock the vocals. The full song plays.
↓
12 / 14
The Finale

Nova
Performs

All 5 layers show up together. You can mute and unmute each one to play around with the mix, then hit Perform. The static breaks apart, color comes flooding back, and Nova takes the stage with the song you built.

ECHO's final line
"I only echoed what was already in you.
You rescued the music. But you made it yours."
